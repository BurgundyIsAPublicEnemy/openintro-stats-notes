# Negative binomial distribution

Describes the probability of observing the k th success on the n th trial.

How many trials it takes to observe a fixed number of successes and require that the last observation be a success.

There are 4 conditions: 

1. Trials are independent
2. Each trial outcome is a success or failure
3. Probability of success (p) is the same for each trial 
4. **Last trial is a success**
